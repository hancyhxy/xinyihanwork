Enhancing E-commerce Replies with Warmth and Personalization

01. Overview

In e-commerce chat scenarios, tone matters as much as accuracy. Most customer-service agents—especially newcomers—struggle to adapt their language style to different users (elderly, children, professionals, etc.). This proposal introduces an AI-driven plug-in that dynamically adjusts tone, empathy level, and language warmth during real-time conversations, helping agents communicate naturally and consistently.

Timeline: Dec 2021 – Mar 2022 · Team: Han Xinyi (Designer), Xu Bai (Designer), Qi Yue (Algorithm Engineer)

02. Problem
  • Inconsistent tone across agents leads to misunderstandings and customer complaints.
  • Switching between communication styles (e.g., formal → friendly) is time-consuming.
  • Existing NLP tools (e.g., Grammarly) optimize grammar, not emotional expression—especially in Chinese conversational language.
03. Opportunity Insight

As chat-based customer service becomes universal, emotional intelligence in text communication is a core differentiator. Our goal was to make empathy scalable through intelligent tone adaptation.

04. Solution

A real-time tone optimization plug-in that corrects and personalizes responses as agents type.

  • While typing, agents can click an “A” icon to instantly review tone.
  • The AI highlights emotional mismatches and suggests replacements—maintaining meaning but improving empathy.
05. Service Mode Setup

Configure tone parameters by audience and context:

  • Audience: Elderly / Children / Women / Men
  • Scenario: E-commerce / Education / Healthcare / Insurance / Government / Concierge
  • Tone: Passionate / Calm / Humorous / Considerate / Lively / Neutral / Serious
  • Add-ons: Sensitive-word alerts & alternative suggestions
06. Live Chat Enhancement

During live chat, the plug-in continuously evaluates tone, highlighting mismatches and proposing style-aligned rewrites without changing intent. This helps agents communicate consistently while saving time.

07. Technical Architecture

Model: Transformer-based text style transfer (BART).

  • Input: Original sentence + language parameters
  • Output: Tone-adjusted sentence
  • Training data: Crowdsourced rewrite pairs under tone labels; machine-mined service dialogues

Pipeline options:

  • Option 1: End-to-end rewrite — faster, data-intensive
  • Option 2: Core-word-based rewrite — more controllable, scalable
08. Competitive Edge
  • Focus: Tone, warmth & empathy (vs. grammar/spelling)
  • Language: Chinese conversational contexts
  • Context: Real-time chat, not long-form documents
  • Output: Emotionally optimized responses with brand-consistent voice
09. Impact
  • Reduced tone-related misunderstandings by automating empathy.
  • Lowered onboarding/training time for new agents.
  • Enabled consistent brand voice across multilingual service environments.
  • Provided groundwork for commercial AI tone-transfer APIs in customer service.
